4

Clean

Spotless homes sell faster. Every surface matters.

"For every hour you spend preparing your home, dedicate 55 minutes inside and 5 minutes outside. Buyers spend more time in the home, so that's where your effort pays off. A beautiful kitchen or fresh paint matters far more than a perfect lawn."

"Today's buyers are savvy and selective. If you're going to remodel, finish it completely before listing. Partial projects raise red flags and create negotiation leverage for buyers. Done is better than perfect."

"I know bright, bold colors express your personality — and that's great! But when it's time to sell, neutral walls give every buyer the blank canvas to imagine their own style. Paint that feature wall a soft gray or warm white. You'll reach more buyers, period."

3

Full Service Marketing

Every one of my listings gets the following:

Professional Photography

High-quality photos are non-negotiable. They're the first impression 95% of buyers get. Our photos highlight your home's best features and sell emotions, not just square footage.

3D Virtual Tours

Let buyers explore your home from their couch. 3D tours increase engagement, reduce showings from unqualified buyers, and create urgency for serious buyers ready to move.

Floor Plans

Clear, professional floor plans help buyers understand layout and flow. They're essential for online listings and give serious buyers everything they need to make decisions.

Single Page Website

Create a personalized domain using your address (like 123Main.com). Paired with an eye-catching sign rider, passersby get instant access to your home's photos, tours, and details right from the street.

Open Houses

Strategic open houses timed to maximize traffic and buyer interest. We'll handle marketing, setup, and coordination so you can relax and focus on making great impressions.

5

Be Transparent About Your Home

Honesty builds trust. Keep detailed records of everything about your home:

Receipts & Invoices from all contractors and vendors

Warranties & Manuals for appliances and systems

Maintenance Records of repairs and updates

Age of Major Systems (roof, HVAC, water heater, etc.)

"Buyers today are informed and inspections are thorough. Be upfront about everything — the good, the bad, and the minor issues. Honesty prevents last-minute negotiations and inspections from derailing the sale. Happy buyers = faster closings at better prices."

6

Stay Flexible & Strategic

"If you can separate selling and buying into two transactions, you'll stress less and negotiate better. When you're not desperate to close by a certain date, you have leverage. You can be selective, wait for better offers, and get better pricing on your next home."

Real estate moves fast. Be ready to adapt — whether that's open houses at unconventional times, accepting contingent offers, or adjusting price strategically. I'll guide every decision to keep YOU in control.
